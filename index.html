Dental Implant

Dental Implantations are titanium posts or screws situated into the jawbone underneath gums through careful arranging. The implant fuses to the bone better as desired. The soft tissues and gums heal more quickly and are less likely to recede. The patient has an immediate restoration, meaning they don't leave the office with missing teeth.
